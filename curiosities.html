Curiosities

1. The word Pokémon is the romanized contraction of the Japanese brand Pocket Monsters.


2. At the beginning there were 150 creatures and today there are more than 700.


3. Pokémon has the accent on the syllable "ké", although many pronounce it on the syllable "mon".


4. Pokémon are a class of creatures inspired by real animals, insects, objects, plants, or mythological creatures.


5. Pokémon is the singular and plural of creatures, however, the RAE pluralizes it as "pokémones".


6. Its creator is Japanese video game designer Satoshi Tajiri.


7. The Pokémon series and movies form a metaserial anime that is based on the video game series (except for Pokémon Yellow).


8. The series motto is ポケモンGETだぜ! (Pokemon Getto Daze!) in Japan, Gotta Catch'em All! in the U.S., catch them now! in Latin America and Get them all! in Spain.


9. Players take on the role of Pokémon trainers.


10. One of the goals of these trainers is to complete the Pokédex by collecting all available Pokémon species that are found.


11. The Pokédex is a portable electronic encyclopedia that serves to automatically record the tokens of all the various Pokémon species seen and captured during the journey as trainers.


12. In the initial stage of the concept its name was "Capsule Monster".


13. There is a Japanese Boeing 747 plane decorated with drawings of Pokémon.


14. A group of researchers from Osaka University discovered a protein that serves to transmit visual information to the brain more quickly and was named "Pikachurina" for the agility of the Pokémon Pikachu.


15. The ultimate goal of the game is to win the Pokémon League and become the regional champion.


16. The theme of collecting, training, and fighting are present in almost all versions of the Pokémon franchise.


17. There are about 18 types in which the pokémon are organized, such as water, fire or grass.


18. Pokémon evolutions are transformations that change and/or improve some of their abilities and characteristics.


19. While most Pokémon have at least one evolution available, there are also Pokémon without an evolutionary chain.


20. Evolution is irreversible, meaning that the evolved Pokémon can never return to its previous stage.
